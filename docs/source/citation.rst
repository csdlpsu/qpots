Citing qPOTS
============

If qPOTS contributes to published work, cite the AISTATS paper that introduces
the algorithm:

   Ashwin Renganathan and Kade Carlson. **qPOTS: Efficient Batch
   Multiobjective Bayesian Optimization via Pareto Optimal Thompson
   Sampling.** Proceedings of the 28th International Conference on Artificial
   Intelligence and Statistics, PMLR 258:4051--4059, 2025.

.. code-block:: bibtex

   @inproceedings{renganathan2025qpots,
     title = {qPOTS: Efficient Batch Multiobjective Bayesian Optimization via
              Pareto Optimal Thompson Sampling},
     author = {Renganathan, Ashwin and Carlson, Kade},
     booktitle = {Proceedings of The 28th International Conference on
                  Artificial Intelligence and Statistics},
     pages = {4051--4059},
     year = {2025},
     editor = {Li, Yingzhen and Mandt, Stephan and Agrawal, Shipra and Khan, Emtiyaz},
     volume = {258},
     series = {Proceedings of Machine Learning Research},
     publisher = {PMLR},
     url = {https://proceedings.mlr.press/v258/renganathan25a.html}
   }

The paper is available from `PMLR
<https://proceedings.mlr.press/v258/renganathan25a.html>`_ and `arXiv
<https://arxiv.org/abs/2310.15788>`_. Repository releases also contain a
machine-readable ``CITATION.cff`` file. The citation metadata will be updated
with the JOSS DOI after the JOSS article is published.
